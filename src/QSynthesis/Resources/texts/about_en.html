About

QSynthesis is the third-party editor of "Vocal synthesis UTAU", which mainly serves the user group of UTAU. It is an independent version of the plug-in QPitchEditor, which can independently edit UTAU project files and operate UTAU voice database.

QSynthesis is open source and shared. It will provide free service for all users for good and update from time to time.

QSynthesis is a front-end software fully compatible with UTAU, which provides services for creators based on the concept of freedom, openness and inclusiveness; the goal is to maintain UTAU's achievements in the field of virtual singers and coexist with UTAU rather than replace UTAU.
